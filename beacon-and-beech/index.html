How it works

In a few steps you can protect precious memories by saving fragile old photos from dust, moisture, fire, and time.

➊ Hand them off

Drop your collection of photos off at our digitization studio in Brookline, MA or arrange pickup for a small fee. Sorting by date will ensure proper order.

➋ Wait while we scan

Wait while each of your prints is carefully scanned, color-corrected, and organized into digital albums. Reverse-side notes are preserved at no additional cost.

➌ Enjoy digital copies

Pick up your originals along with a USB drive of high-resolution digital photos. You can now enjoy your collection on any device or share them on social media.

Common Questions and Answers

Trusting another party with your media requires trust. We want to make sure you feel confident and secure in every step of the process.

How do you get my digital copies back to me?

Your copies will be handed off on a secure USB thumb drive when you receive your originals. From there you can copy your photos on to your PC or Mac to save additional copies or share with friends and family.

Do I get to keep my originals?

Of course. Your original prints will be returned in the same condition they were delivered to the Beacon & Beech studio.

Can you digitize my slides, video tapes, or film?

The Beacon & Beech studio can currently only handle prints but we hope to expand to more physical media in the future.

How long will it take?

Most projects can be delivered in 7-10 business days. Compare this to services such as LegacyBox or DigMyPhotos which promise 6-8 weeks.

Can I send my album books?

All photos must be delivered loose, ideally in plastic bags or envelopes.

Can you arrange my photos by date?

We will process your photos in the order they are shared.

Where and by whom are my photos scanned?

All scanning is done proudly in Brookline, MA. Your photos are handled with care by operator Bram Oosten who has 8+ years of experience in photo digitization. You can see some of his digitization work here.

Do you take COVID-19 precautions?

Yes, all handoffs are no-contact and photos are handled with gloves.
