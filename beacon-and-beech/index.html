How it works

In a few steps you can protect precious memories by saving fragile old photos from dust, moisture, fire, and time.

➊ Handoff

Drop your collection of photos off at our digitization studio in Brookline, MA or arrange pickup for a small fee. Sorting by date will ensure proper order.

➋ Scanning

Wait while each of your prints is carefully scanned, color-corrected, and organized into digital albums. Reverse-side notes are preserved at no additional cost.

➌ Delivery

Pick up your originals along with a USB drive of high-resolution digital photos. You can now enjoy your collection on any device or share them on social media.
